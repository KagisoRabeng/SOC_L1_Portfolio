# Humans as Attack Vectors
## Overview

This room focuses on how attackers exploit human behavior as an entry point into organizations. It introduces common social engineering techniques and highlights why users are often considered the weakest link in security.

Path: SOC Level 1

Difficulty: Easy

Focus Area: Social Engineering, User Awareness, Threat Vectors

## What Was Covered

- Social engineering concepts
- Phishing and pretexting attacks
- Human error as a security risk
- Importance of security awareness training

## Key Points

- Attackers frequently target people instead of systems
- Phishing is one of the most common initial access techniques
- Lack of awareness increases organizational risk
- Users play a critical role in early threat detection

## Takeaways

- Understanding human-based attack vectors helps SOC analysts:

- Support awareness and prevention initiatives
