# Phishing Fundamentals

## Overview
This sub-room introduces phishing as a common initial access technique used by attackers. It explains how phishing attacks work, the different types of phishing, and why they are a major focus area for SOC teams.

**Path:** SOC Level 1  
  
**Focus Area:** Phishing, Social Engineering, Email Security

---

## What Was Covered
- Definition of phishing
- Common phishing techniques
- Types of phishing attacks
- Role of the SOC in phishing detection

---

## Types of Phishing
- **Email Phishing** – Mass-distributed malicious emails
- **Spear Phishing** – Targeted phishing attacks
- **Whaling** – Targeting executives or high-value users
- **Smishing** – Phishing via SMS
- **Vishing** – Phishing via voice calls

---

## Key Points
- Phishing is a leading cause of security incidents
- Attackers exploit trust and urgency
- Email is the most common phishing vector
- User reports are critical for early detection
