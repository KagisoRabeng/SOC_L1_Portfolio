# Phishing Prevention

## Overview
This sub-room focuses on preventing phishing attacks through technical controls, user awareness, and SOC processes. It explains how organizations reduce phishing risk before and after emails reach users.

**Path:** SOC Level 1  
  
**Focus Area:** Phishing Prevention, Defensive Security



## What Was Covered
- Purpose of phishing prevention
- Technical and non-technical controls
- Role of the SOC in prevention efforts
- Reducing phishing impact



## Prevention Techniques
- Email filtering and spam controls
- DMARC, SPF, and DKIM
- User security awareness training
- Reporting and response workflows



## Key Points
- Prevention reduces incident volume
- Users are a critical line of defense
- Email authentication improves trust
- SOC teams support continuous improvement
